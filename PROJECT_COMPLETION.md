## 🎯 Features Implemented

### Feed Algorithms (5 Types)

```
🔥 HOT (Default)
   Formula: (likes × 2) + comments + (reposts × 1.5)
   Shows: Best mix of popular and fresh

🕐 NEW
   Shows: Latest posts first

⬆️ TOP
   Shows: Most upvoted posts

✨ BEST
   Formula: likes - dislikes + (comments × 0.5)
   Shows: Quality-ranked content

💣 CONTROVERSIAL
   Formula: min(likes, dislikes)
   Shows: Highly debated/polarizing content
```
